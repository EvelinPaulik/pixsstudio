  • Chiro Center

Brief

From concept development, to design, to web and eCommerce store.

ChiroCenter is a leading chiropractic and wellness service in North America, paving the way for wellness and technology together. ChiroCenter needed a unique tech solution that showcased business reviews, live customer chat, and up to date news articles.

Launch Site
Services Provided:
  • Wordpress Integration
  • Content Management Integration
  • Art Direction
  • Design, Illustration
  • Marketing
  • Seo Analysis

Desktop

01/ Concepts

Our mission was to creating a unique experience which combined information, articles, and health data with a user friendly and appealing interface.

02/ Modules

Live customer chat and authomated business reviews integrating Google API were of key importance conveying business value and strategic direction

03/ Platform

Utilizing a Content Management System allowed ChiroCenter to be notified of live chats, full control of business reviews, and easy website operation proccess.




If advertisers spent the same amount of money on improving their products as they do on advertising then they wouldn't have to advertise them.

- Paul Rogers

ChiroCenter features a chiropractic and wellness service website strong on content and brand information. The technical integration of different live features allows customers to have more business information on their fingertips, building a strong branding and company trust.

Our focus for this website creation project was all about further building the brand of ChiroCenter and leading users to build trust within the brand.
