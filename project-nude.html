Brief

eCommerce business growth and sales analytics with a focus on scalability.

Nude is a small makeup business with a emphasis on creativity, beauty, and natural purity, while promoting health and well-being. The project implemented a fully responsive solution backed by a robust Content Management System and Analytics Dashboard and Reporting which allowed the client to increase website traffic and views, gain information from leads and prospects, and brand their business.

Launch Site
Services Provided:
  • Wordpress Integration
  • Content Management Integration
  • Anlytics Reporting
  • Marketing
  • Seo Analysis

Desktop

01/ Leads and Client Data

Finding leads is one of the most important aspects of a small business. Through showcasing their brand and gathering client phone numbers and emails Nude was able to gather 6x the amount of leads.

02/ Search Engine Optimization

A Search Engine Optimized (SEO) website is key to finding clients and sales. By focusing on google keywords and website optimization Nude was able to reach 352% more organic traffic.

03/ Business Branding

As a small business, Nude was able to set themselves apart from the competition by establishing who you are and what you represent through their website. Business sales increase by 221% over the first month of launch.
